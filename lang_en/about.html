Winking
Corporation

Winking Corporation was established in 2003, dedicated ever since to the creation of moving stories and high-quality visuals. With over 17 years of game development and art outsourcing experience, our dedication and professionalism are a guarantee of top quality for our partners' projects.

Currently, Winking Corporation has over 400 employees through Taipei, Shanghai, Nanjing, and Manila. Our expertise includes art outsourcing and full project development, across PC, Mobile, Switch, Xbox One, PS4, and the upcoming PS5.

Giving Back

Winking values compassion and kindness in life. We encourage our staff to go out into the community and be part of something greater. Winking also participated in several awareness movements in many parts of the world, includes Beijing, Florida, Taipei, and more. We work closely with an orphanage in Changchun, China to support children with school supplies and warm clothes. We also collaborate with local elderly care to bring them commodities.

Calvin Shen, Florida

I had the honor to volunteer at a local daycare center for two years. It was great to give back to the community. Getting to see all the children every week was rewarding. What I love the most was seeing their smile after a long day of work. They would come up to me and ask me about the many adventures that I took on during work. I always entertained them with my stories. I am glad to be part of their lives.

Christine Lo, Beijing

I have volunteered for migrant school project for half a year when I lived in Beijing. We visited the school 1~2 days per week. Our main job was to parent these students and to teach basic English conversation and Mathematics. This volunteer work has taught me the importance of universal education and caring. More children should go to school and have better education no matter what social class their parents are.

Vivian Huang, Taipei

In Taiwan, eating reunion dinner with family on New Year’s Eve is a very important custom. However, it’s hard for some elderly people living alone to have a reunion dinner. I joined a “deliver New Year dishes and keep the elderly living alone company” program. I realized that a simple care and the dishes really brightened up their New Year’s Eve while seeing the smile on their faces.

Investors

Over the years, investors of Winking Corporation include established and influential companies, such as Intel and HSBC in 2006, SBI Holdings from Japan, as well as UMC, Cathay Holdings, Taishin Holdings, SinoPac Holdings and more.

Audited by the Big Four for the past decades, our investors and auditors are a testimony to the strict standards of management Winking Entertainment practices.

Winking has been awarded the Top 10 of Asia Technology Fast 50 by the Deloitte Fast Program, and Top 100 Asia from Red Herring.
